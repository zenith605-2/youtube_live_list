Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-10

What this site does

This site indexes publicly available YouTube videos and live streams (CCTV/webcam feeds, dashcam footage, wildlife and crowd footage, etc.) and organizes them by category. We do not host, download, or redistribute any video content — every entry links directly to the original video on YouTube.

Information we collect

  • Account info: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google, stored via our database provider (Supabase) to identify your submissions, reports, upvotes, and favorites.
  • Activity data: Links you submit, reports you file, upvotes you give, favorites and notes you save, and your daily free-view usage are stored to operate the site's community features.
  • No video content is collected or stored. We only store metadata (title, channel, thumbnail URL, category) about publicly available YouTube videos.

Cookies and advertising

This site may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party services

  • YouTube / Google: embedded video playback is subject to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: our database and authentication provider, subject to the Supabase Privacy Policy.

Your choices

You can use this site without signing in — sign-in is only required to submit links, report issues, favorite items, or unlock extra daily views. You can delete your account data by contacting us.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised through the site's Feedback & Feature Requests board or via the site's GitHub repository issue tracker.
